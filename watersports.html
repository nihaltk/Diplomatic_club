watersports

With perpetual sunshine in the bay, fun opportunities in the water are endless. From surfboarding to banana rides, there’s a plethora of activities to choose from. Based on the sheltered waters of our beach, we boast trained individuals to guide you through your adventure of watersports and our visually stunning surroundings of Doha's most exciting and up-and-coming areas. We give you access to our finest boards, boots and lifejackets to safeguard yourself. Our watersports club is your first port of call for all things related to fun in the water!

WATER SPORTS

Whether you are a beginner or an experienced water sportsman, we can accommodate your needs. Choose from water skiing, wakeboarding, banana boat and tube rides.

TERMS AND CONDITIONS

  • Our first priority is your safety, hence all of our watersports guides have been certified by coastguards.
  • Life jackets must be worn at all times.
  • All safety equipment is in accordance to international standards.
  • Do not take any valuable items with you as kayaks and sailboats are prone to capsizing.
  • Be aware that the sea can be rough and there is danger from passing motorboats.
