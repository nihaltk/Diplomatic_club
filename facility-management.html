DCCFMS BRIEF

The demand for The Diplomatic Club’s impeccable services reached grand proportions which led to providing general services to both the private and government sector. By as early as 2005, The Diplomatic Club originated the Diplomatic Club Facility Management System, born out of the need for a dedicated section providing the cream in facilities management.
Starting with a modest team in 2007, we now boast a highly trained and capable staff of 1000 individuals equipped with the entire range of expertise. It is this expertise that allows us to operate in over 55 locations in Qatar.
We believe in maintaining our reputation through our work. The DCFMS is the final word in cafeteria operations, industrial catering, recreation, office assistant services, housekeeping services, receptionist, call center services and more. Our F&B team on the other hand is the culmination of 20 years of experience combined with a creative flair and accurate pricing. Our operational services cover an entire range of activities under preventive and reactive building maintenance along with being superlative in augmentative services that include pest control, waste management, and security – the works.
Our team that manages it all, comprises a certain handful of talented individuals in Qatar, committed to bringing professionalism and efficiency to every aspect of their work. Client benefit coupled with loyalty to our work is the principal motto of The Diplomatic Club Facilities Management System.
