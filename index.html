About Us

The Diplomatic Club is an intimate, serene waterfront club in the heart of West Bay. Our vision tastefully combines quality and extravagance, infused with a rustic Middle Eastern charm that will leave you wanting more. From dining to leisure, our hospitality extends to families, friends and business personnel.

Connoisseurs in our kitchen present their expertise in gourmet meals in all of our four extraordinary restaurants and at the banquet and catering events where class and elegance surpass. Whether you choose to dine at one of our restaurants or reserve a meeting at one of our event spaces, they all provide the luxury of indoor and outdoor seating.

Enhance your lifestyle with an agenda for physical fitness followed by a rejuvenating session at the spa. Dive into our outdoor pool, while we rustle up a refreshing mocktail & snack or simply slip comfortably into sunbeds that adorn our beach. That’s not it! Get a makeover with our team of experts at the ladies or gents salon.

Last but not the least, with an urge to curb the growing demand for a qualitative facilities management system, the Diplomatic Club Facilities Management Services (DCFMS) was formed with the initiative to establish innovative business practices.

Membership

The Diplomatic Club was founded with an aim to bring together a community that values the decorum of luxury.

We are delighted to provide our patrons with two membership choices:

  • Corporate Membership
  • Private Membership

With much of our rates built on exclusivity, our members can indulge themselves in a complete array of facilities ranging from culinary delights, well-crafted events, salon services and recreational facilities. A member affiliated to this program can utilize their membership for themselves and their guests to gain access to our hallmark style of perfection that is in harmony with positive vibes, great food, fitness, beauty makeovers and generous hospitality.

Let our dedicated account manager guide you through the privileges of our membership program.

Recreation

Distancing ourselves from the hustling West Bay, our recreation area is designed for both being active and kicking back. Our guests can be in high spirits and awaken their senses in a collection of spaces designed to blur the boundary between the city and the club.

Our recreation area features an outdoor swimming pool, a poolside restaurant, fitness classes, gym studios, and a private beach. We have the expertise and facilities to create a bespoke program to meet your personal goals. Offerings by our recreation include goal setting fitness experiences, detox water, fresh fruit and a steam & sauna room – all designed to counter the stresses of life. Our team is always eager to help, whatever your enquiry, so if you're wondering what session is right for you to book, you'll find our team ready and willing to guide you!

Spa

Holistic head-to-toe spa therapies coupled with impeccable attention to luxury, make it a restorative oasis to help you de-stress, rejuvenate, and pamper yourself.
