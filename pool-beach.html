POOL AND BEACH CLUB

Even at the peak of summer, with the thermostat all the way up, our temperature controlled outdoor pool at the club has you covered. Take a plunge at our family-friendly pool, where the experience of ultimate relaxation and rejuvenation is all that matters. Enjoy poolside mocktails and meal service – all within the comfort of your lounger. Satisfying every sunbather, The Diplomatic Club features a private beach club within the premises. Guests can enjoy private access to the beach club with sun-loungers, umbrellas, towels and food & beverage service.

SWIMMING LESSONS

If you’re frightened of the water or think you’re too old to swim, we may be just what you’re looking for. Swimming is one of the few sports that can build stamina, flexibility and strength while placing strain on the heart and joints. This is due to the water supporting the majority of the swimmer’s body weight.

Lessons available for beginners, intermediates and advanced - children & adults.

Our swimming coach will help you to achieve your goals!
