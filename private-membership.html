PRIVATE MEMBERSHIP PRIVILEGES

  • Access to pristine recreational facilities that includes: A new state of the art fitness facility, swimming pool, steam room, sauna, jacuzzi, plunge pool, showers & lockers, kids' club, watersports and beach access.
  • Access to sport activities: beach volleyball, beach handball, beach football, basketball, badminton, outdoor tennis court, squash court, table tennis and various fun activities.
  • Enjoy catering services, rent banquet halls and dine at all our Food and Beverage outlets - Al Sayyad, Nassayem Qatar, Al Borj and Member's Lounge with a 20% discount on Food & Beverages.
  • 20% discount at the Beauty Center and Men’s Salon on all services except on treatments and salon products (the discount on services is applicable if the amount is above QR 60)
  • Complimentary aromatherapy massage upon enrolling as a member.
  • One welcome voucher at Nassayem Qatar worth QR 250 (For family and couple memberships) and QR 125 (For single membership), will be presented upon enrolling as a member that can be availed on the weekdays.
  • Discounted rates on:
    Personal training
    Watersports
    Indoor & Outdoor sports classes
    Massages
    Daily entrance fees

Every member will have the ability to use their membership card as a credit charge card with a ceiling limit of Qr. 3000. This can be availed by showing your membership card and signing the bill; the debit amount of the invoice will be added to the respective members' membership account at the end of the month.
